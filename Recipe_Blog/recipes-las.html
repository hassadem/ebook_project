Classic Lasagna

Layers of pasta, ricotta, mozzarella, and meat sauce make this lasagna a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Meat sauce

Instructions

  1. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente.
  2. Layer noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, and meat sauce in a baking dish.
  3. Repeat layers and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30 minutes.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
